ROLLS ROYCE

Rolls Royce

Rolls-Royce is a British luxury automobile maker that has been operating as a subsidiary of BMW since 2003. The company is known for its high-end vehicles like the Phantom, Cullinan, and Ghost. Rolls-Royce also has a history in aerospace and defense, with Rolls-Royce Holdings plc being a separate multinational entity focused on power systems for aviation and other industries.
